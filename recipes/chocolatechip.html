Ghirardelli Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups unsifted flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup shortening
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup walnuts, chopped (optional)
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Process

  1. Beat butter and sugars at medium speed until creamy and light In color (with hand mixer: about 4 minutes; with Bosch: watch for color change, only 1-2 minutes).
  2. Add vanilla and eggs, one at a time. Mix on low only till incorporated.
  3. Add dry ingredients, pre-mixed together with fork.
  4. Stir in chocolate and nuts.
  5. Bake at 370˚ for 9-10 minutes. Just till golden. Don’t overbake
