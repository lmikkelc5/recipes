Sourdough Bread

Ingredients

  • 450g Bread Flour
  • 300g Water
  • 100g Sourdough Starter
  • 10g Salt

Process

  1. Combine all ingredients and knead until combined.
  2. Cover and let rise for 1 1/2, "stretch and fold" every 30 min
  3. After 3rd "stretch and fold" leave to rise for 1 1/2 hours to 3 1/2 hours more.
  4. After rising remove from bowl and shape into a ball. Dust with flour and cover with towel for 30 min.
  5. After letting rest shape into desired shape and place in bannetone basket, cover and put in the fridge to proof
  6. Leave to proof for 8 hours or more.
  7. Remove from fridge 30 min prior to baking and place on parchment paper, preheat over to 450 with dutch oven inside.
  8. Score after 30 min and move parchment paper into dutch oven. Cook for 20 min with lid on, then remove lid and let cook until it reaches desired color(10-15 min for me)
